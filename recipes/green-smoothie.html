Strawberry green smoothie

Description

A delish smoothy filled with strawberry and green veggies

Ingredients

  • 120g ripe strawberries
  • 140g baby spinach
  • 1 avocado, scooped
  • 100ml quark

Steps

  1. Put all the ingredients in a blender and whizz until completely smooth.
