Strawberry green smoothie

Description

Try this tasty breakfast smoothie to start your day, pairing sweet fruit with nutritious spinach and avocado for a dose of calcium, vitamin C, folate and fibre (original recipe source)

Ingredients

  • 160g ripe strawberries, hulled
  • 160g baby spinach
  • 1 small avocado, halved and the flesh scooped out
  • 150ml pot bio yogurt
  • 2 small oranges, juiced, plus ½ tsp finely grated zest

Steps

  1. Put all the ingredients in a blender and whizz until completely smooth.
  2. If it’s a little thick, add a drop of chilled water then blitz again.
  3. Pour into glasses and drink straight away.
