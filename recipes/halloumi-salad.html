Halloumi Salad

Description

A lovely beetroot, halloumi and pomegrenate dish with some pumpkin seed crunch (original recipe source)

Ingredients

  • thinly sliced half red onion
  • 2 tblsp apple cider vinegar
  • 2 oranges
  • rocket leaves
  • 2 cooked and chopped beetroots
  • rapeseed oil
  • 80g halloumi (thin halved slices)
  • 80g pomegrenate seeds
  • 2 tbsp pumpkin seeds
  • mint leaves
  • dill

Steps

  1. Put the sliced onion in a bowl, add the vinegar and toss well.
  2. Cut the peel and pith from the orange then segment over the onion bowl to make sure that you catch any juice.
  3. Pile the rocket onto plates and dot over the beetroot and orange.
  4. Rub a drop of oil around a non-stick frying pan then fry the halloumi in the pan for about 30 seconds each side until golden.
  5. Stir the pomegranate into the onions and spoon this and all the dressing, over the rocket.
  6. Then top with the halloumi and scatter with the pumpkin seeds, mint and dill.
